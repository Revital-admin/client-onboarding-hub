MAKE PROPOSAL FOLLOW-UP TRACKER + CONTRACT & INVOICE TRACKER STANDALONE
=========================================================================

WHAT CHANGED AND WHY
-----------------------------------
You noticed the Proposal Follow-Up Tracker's client dropdown was pulling
from the same client list as the rest of the Hub - meaning to track a
follow-up for someone who hasn't signed yet, you'd have to create them
as a full Client Workspace first. Same issue applied to Contract &
Invoice Tracker (a contract often goes out before someone's a real
client too).

Both tools are now standalone: type a name directly (with autocomplete
suggestions pulled from your existing clients, for the occasional
upsell/renewal proposal to someone you already work with) - no need to
create a Client Workspace just to track a proposal or contract. Each
keeps its own list in the cloud, so nothing gets mixed into your real
client data.

Client Renewal Tracker was left as-is - that one is specifically about
renewing an existing signed client, so it makes sense for it to stay
tied to your real client list.

HEADS UP - EXISTING DATA
-------------------------------------------------------------------
If you already have proposals or contracts being tracked against real
Client Workspaces from before this change, that data lives inside
those clients' records and won't automatically show up in the new
standalone lists (they're reading from a different place now). If you
had anything actively tracked in either tool, let me know and I'll
write a one-time migration to carry it over - otherwise you're starting
both trackers fresh from a clean list.
