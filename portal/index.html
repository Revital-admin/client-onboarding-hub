Campaign Briefs — What to Expect

Before we produce anything, we create a Campaign Brief outlining the objective, audience, message, creative direction, platforms, and timeline. When a brief arrives:

StepWhat to Do
1. ReviewReview the brief in full — check the objective, messaging, and direction
2. Question or CommentLeave any questions or feedback directly in the brief task
3. ConfirmConfirm approval with your account manager

⚠️ Production does not begin until the brief is approved — the sooner you review it, the sooner your content is in production.

How to Submit Feedback & Revisions

When a deliverable is ready for your review, you'll receive an email with a link to the content and a Content Approval Form. Complete the form and return it within 48 hours. The form has three options:

OptionWhat Happens
✅ ApprovedWe publish on the scheduled date — no further action needed
🔄 Approved with Minor CorrectionsNote the changes and we apply them before publishing
❌ Revision RequiredWe action your feedback and send a revised version

🔒 Verbal approvals are not accepted. A completed approval form is required before any content is published.

All feedback must come through the portal — not via email, text, or phone. Use the Submit a Revision button in Quick Actions, name the task clearly, write your feedback as specifically as possible, and set a priority.

❌ Vague✅ Specific
"I don't like it""Can we change the font color to our brand blue?"
"Change the copy""The headline feels too formal — try something more conversational"
"The image is wrong""Can we use something with natural lighting and a warmer tone?"
"Fix the video""At 0:23 the text is too small on mobile — increase the font size"

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I know when something needs my review?

You'll get an email notification the same day it's ready. You can also check Active Projects for anything awaiting your review.

Can I send feedback via email or text?

Please use the portal only, via the Submit a Revision or Submit Content Request buttons in Quick Actions. That keeps everything in one trackable place.

How many revisions am I allowed?

Your revision rounds are outlined in your Statement of Work. Additional rounds beyond what's included may require a Change Order — your account manager will let you know.

How do I send you files or brand assets?

Use the Upload Files button in Quick Actions. No login needed — just fill in your details, describe what you're uploading, and attach your files. We'll confirm receipt within 1 business day.

Can I submit ideas for content or posts?

Yes — use the Submit Content Request button in Quick Actions. Tell us your idea, the platform, and any inspiration you have. We'll follow up within 2 business days.

What if I want to add a new service or change my plan?

Email your account manager — they'll prepare a proposal or Change Order. No scope changes happen without written agreement from both sides.
